about us

   |  2 minutes  |  248 words  |  Roberto  • Other languages: Deutsch

the Eigenbaukombinat in halle (saale) is a co-workshop - also known as open workshop. that is to say that we operate as a unit equipped with tools and machines, which can be used equally by each member. on this web page we inform about our workshop, projects of our members and our for all open organizations. if you are interested, just come by us during our opening times. If you want to get to know a lot of members at once, then the knowledge and cooking evening is a perfect starting point. [Read More]
